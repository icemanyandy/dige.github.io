人脸检测/Face detection

检测照片中的人脸——使用从大量人脸图像数据中发展出来的机器学习技术，快速准确地识别人脸。我们的技术可以检测多个人脸。此外，它还可以在一定程度上检测出类似人类的面孔，如卡通或CG人物，甚至狗和猫的面孔。Detect faces in photos — recognize faces quickly and accurately, using machine learning technologies developed from massive sets of facial image data. Our technology can detect multiple faces. Additionally, it can detect human-like faces, such as cartoon or CG characters, and even dog and cat faces, to some degree.

人脸分析/Face analytics


每一张脸都是由眼睛、鼻子、嘴和其他部分组成的，没有两张脸是完全相同的。使用我们独特的面部分析算法，我们可以量化各种面部参数。我们的技术还可以分析各种属性，如性别、年龄或情感。Every face is composed of eyes, a nose, a mouth, and other parts, and no two faces are exactly the same.Using our unique facial analysis algorithms, we can quantify various facial parameters.Our technology can also analyze various attributes, such as gender, age, or emotion.
